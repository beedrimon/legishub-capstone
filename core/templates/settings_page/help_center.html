Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a new document?

Click the "Upload" button located in the top-right corner of the Documents page. Fill in the required metadata and select your PDF file, then click "Upload".

What happens when a document is archived?

Archiving a document permanently moves it out of the active "Documents" repository and into the "Archives". This action tracks retention policies and secures the historical record.

Why can't I edit certain documents?

If you are logged in as a Legislator, you only have "Read-Only" access and cannot modify or upload records. Only Encoders (Staff) and Admins have edit privileges.

How does Auto-Approve work?

Any document left in the "For Approval" status for more than 14 days will be automatically approved and transferred to the Archives by the system's background routine. Staff will be notified via email.
